### Safecut Network Effect: DeFi MVP Launch Plan (0-6 Months)

#### **Phase 1: "Район Північний" - Local Market Capture (0-6 Months)**

**Objective:** Establish the Safecut Network in the local market by creating a strong community and generating initial demand for the SFC token.

---

### Week 1-4: Pre-Launch Preparation
1. **Website & Social Media Setup**
   - Develop a user-friendly website with information about Safecut, the SFC token, and how to participate.
   - Set up social media profiles on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok.

2. **Tokenomics Whitepaper**
   - Publish a detailed whitepaper outlining the tokenomics of SFC, including total supply, distribution, utility, and burn mechanisms.

3. **Partnerships & Collaborations**
   - Identify local barbershops, salons, and businesses that can accept SFC as payment.
   - Secure agreements with these partners to create a network effect from day one.

4. **Marketing Materials**
   - Design flyers, posters, and digital ads highlighting the benefits of using SFC.
   - Create engaging content for social media, including videos showcasing the Safecut experience and testimonials from early adopters.

---

### Week 5-8: Soft Launch & Community Building
1. **Soft Launch Event**
   - Host a soft launch event at the main barbershop to introduce the SFC token.
   - Invite local influencers, community leaders, and media to generate buzz.

2. **Community Engagement**
   - Start a Discord or Telegram group for Safecut Network members to discuss the project, share experiences, and provide feedback.
   - Organize regular Q&A sessions with the team to address any questions and build trust.

3. **Referral Program Launch**
   - Introduce the "Refer-a-Friend" program, where users earn SFC tokens by referring new clients.
   - Promote this program through social media and in-store signage.

4. **Loyalty Program Integration**
   - Implement a loyalty program that rewards frequent customers with SFC tokens.
   - Offer exclusive discounts and perks to members of the Safecut Network.

---

### Week 9-12: Marketing Campaigns & FOMO Creation
1. **Limited-Time Offers**
   - Launch limited-time offers for new clients, such as free services or discounted rates when paying with SFC.
   - Create a sense of urgency by setting expiration dates on these offers.

2. **Social Media Ads**
   - Run targeted social media ads to reach potential customers in the local area.
   - Highlight the benefits of using SFC and the unique features of the Safecut Network.

3. **Influencer Partnerships**
   - Collaborate with local influencers, such as barbers, beauty bloggers, and fitness trainers, to promote the project.
   - Encourage them to share their experiences with the Safecut Network on social media.

4. **Community Events**
   - Organize community events, such as open houses, charity shaves, or wellness workshops, to engage with potential customers.
   - Use these events to educate people about the benefits of using SFC and encourage them to join the network.

---

### Week 13-24: Expansion & Network Effect
1. **Onboarding New Partners**
   - Continue expanding the network by onboarding more local businesses that can accept SFC as payment.
   - Offer incentives for these partners, such as free marketing support or exclusive discounts on services.

2. **Safecut CRM Integration**
   - Integrate a CRM system to manage customer data and streamline operations.
   - Use this system to track transactions, rewards, and referrals within the network.

3. **Data Analysis & Optimization**
   - Collect and analyze data from the first few months of operation to identify trends and areas for improvement.
   - Optimize marketing campaigns and loyalty programs based on this data to maximize engagement and retention.

4. **Feedback Loop**
   - Establish a feedback loop with customers to gather insights and suggestions for improving the Safecut Network.
   - Use this feedback to make continuous improvements and address any issues that arise.

---

### Week 25-26: Final Preparations & Hard Launch
1. **Final Testing**
   - Conduct thorough testing of all systems, including payment gateways, loyalty programs, and CRM software.
   - Ensure that everything is functioning smoothly before the hard launch.

2. **Press Release & Media Coverage**
   - Issue a press release announcing the official launch of the Safecut Network.
   - Reach out to local media outlets for coverage, including newspapers, radio stations, and online publications.

3. **Hard Launch Event**
   - Host a grand opening event at the main barbershop to celebrate the official launch of the Safecut Network.
   - Invite guests, partners, and community members to join in the celebration and experience the benefits of using SFC.

4. **Continuous Marketing Efforts**
   - Maintain consistent marketing efforts through social media, email newsletters, and local advertising.
   - Keep the momentum going by introducing new promotions and incentives as needed.
